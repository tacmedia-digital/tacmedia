My Design Process

1

Discovery

I start by understanding your business goals, target audience, and project requirements. This involves research, competitor analysis, and establishing clear objectives for your web design.

2

Wireframing

Creating the blueprint of your website with wireframes that outline the structure, layout, and key elements. This allows us to focus on functionality before aesthetics.

3

Visual Design

Developing the visual identity with color schemes, typography, imagery, and other design elements that align with your brand and appeal to your audience.

4

Refinement

Iterating on the design based on your feedback until we achieve the perfect look and feel for your website. This ensures the final product meets your expectations.
